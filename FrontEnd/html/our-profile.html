HS Lanka Pvt Ltd — Setting a higher standard in maritime services

Founded in 2018 in Colombo, Sri Lanka, HS Lanka Pvt Ltd delivers swift, responsive, and cost-effective ship agency and maritime services for cargo, cruise, tanker, and bulk vessels calling Sri Lankan waters. We combine proven project management practices with a global supplier network to ensure seamless coordination, reduced turnaround times, and measurable savings — 24/7/365.

  • End-to-end agency support: port formalities, husbandry, and logistics
  • Round-the-clock ship supply & spares logistics with rapid response
  • Trusted, long-term partnerships focused on reliability and value
